Social Committee Updates and Upcoming Events


We had a great night out together in May at the Crooked Cue. We had almost 100% participation and very positive feedback. We'll continue to build on this with big and small events that are coming very soon. Here's a sneak peak:


  • Toronto Office Summer Party at Cabana: Thursday July 20th! We will have full details and a formal invite going out soon. This event will go all afternoon and will feature BBQ, Beach Volleyball, mini-golf and dirving range or just relaxing in one of the Cabana lounges beside the water! Cabana is located 5 min from Downtown

  • June Birthdays: We'll be celebrating the June Babies with something sweet on the 7th Floor on Thurs June 22nd. We fully expect the Geminis to push to the Cancers aside to get to the front of the line.

  • Canada 150: We will be decorating the office and having some treats on Thursday June 29th to celebrate Canada Day.

  • As always, be on the lookout for random acts of coffee and pizza.

Have a great Canada Day Weekend!
